# Business Model

### Featured ads
* `X amount of money`
* `Unlimited amount of featured ads`
* `2 week period`
* `If the ad expires within the featured period it's extended to the length of it`

### Premium ads
* `Y amount of money`
* `Limited amount of premium ads`
* `2 week period`
* `If the ad expires within the premium period it's extended to the length of it`

### Refresh an ad
* `Z amount of money`
* `Refreshes ad expiration date`
* `Indirectly puts it back to top of the list`

### Banners
* `External related advertisements`
* `1 month period`
